How Using RainDrop helps the planet

E-waste Prevented (kg): The average e-waste volume generated by a standard desktop computer is between 9-23kg. We use 12kg as an estimate, taking into account the average workstation usage is 5 years, and the average current workstation is 2.5 years old. Then we take into account the total time all of your RainDrops have been in use, and that using a RainDrop client doubles the effective usefulness of your workstations to calculate the weight of the total e-waste prevented. Reference

Electricity Saved (KWh): The average desktop power supply is 300W, and the average laptop charger is 60W. We use 100W as the estimated potential savings by utilizing a cloud workstation due to the feasibility of using a smart monitor, zero-client, or mobile device as the main connection point to the RainDrop.

Resources Preserved: Reference

  • Water (L): It takes about 400 gallons to make one computer: Reference. Each RainDrop saves using 1/5 of the water of a computer yearly (80 gallons = 300 liters)/5 years = 60L/year saved.
  • Metals (kg): Each 20% of 12kg for the average computer = 2.4 so 2.4/5 years = 0.48kg/year saved.
  • Plastics (kg): Each 35% of 12kg for the average computer = 4.2 so 4.2/5 years = 0.84kg/year saved.
